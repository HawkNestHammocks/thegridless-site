Quiz Formats

Multiple Ways to Learn

Different question types keep your brain engaged and improve retention from different angles.

Multiple Choice

Classic format — pick the right answer from 4 options. Quick, effective, and great for knowledge testing.

What's the universal signal for distress?
Two whistle blasts
Three of anything (fires, whistle blasts, etc.)
One long whistle blast

True or False

Quick-fire knowledge checks. Challenge common myths and misconceptions about wilderness survival.

Moss always grows on the north side of trees.
True
False — it depends on moisture, not direction

Scenario-Based

Real-world situations that test your judgment. "You're lost in the woods at dusk — what do you do first?"

Yes, clear water is safe
No — always purify first, even clear water can contain pathogens

Identification

Identify plants, knots, tracks, and more. Visual recognition skills that could save your life in the field.

Joining two ropes together
