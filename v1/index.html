Test Yourself

What Would You Do?

Tap each scenario. Be honest — would you know the answer without looking it up?

1
You're hiking and encounter a bear. Do you run, play dead, or fight back?
It depends on the species. Brown/grizzly bear — play dead. Black bear — make yourself big and fight back. Running triggers chase instinct in all bears. Gridless covers this in detail.
2
The power's been out for 48 hours. Your water supply is running low. How do you purify water?
Boiling (1 minute at rolling boil), chemical treatment (bleach: 8 drops per gallon), UV light, or filtration. Each method has trade-offs. Learn all 7 methods in Gridless.
3
You're lost in the woods with no compass or GPS. How do you find north?
Use the sun (rises in east, sets in west), find the North Star at night, use a stick shadow method, or check moss growth (unreliable but useful). Gridless teaches 6 navigation methods.
4
Someone in your group has a deep cut that won't stop bleeding. No medical kit. What do you do?
Apply direct pressure with the cleanest cloth available. Elevate the wound above the heart. If arterial bleeding, apply a tourniquet 2-3 inches above the wound. Full first aid guide in Gridless.
